I'm Christian Coutinho, a senior IT and cybersecurity executive with over 25 years of experience in leadership roles within the ICT field, working across different industries and multicultural companies.

I currently serve as CISO (Senior Information Security Associate Director) at **B3 — Brasil, Bolsa, Balcão**, the largest stock exchange in Latin America, where I lead cybersecurity strategy and the security transformation program based on the NIST 2.0, CIS, and ISO 27001 frameworks. Previously, I was **Global CISO at LATAM Airlines Group**, in Chile, leading global information security operations across 25 countries.

I'm an *ISA PCI-DSS Certified Assessor* and a member of organizations including the PCI-SSC Regional Engagement Board, CyberEDBoard, CIONET, Brazilian Aviation Security Team (BASeT), and ANPPD (National Association of Data Privacy Professionals). In 2022 and 2023, I was recognized by HotTopics.ht as one of the top 100 CISOs worldwide, and in 2023 I received the *CISO of the Year* award from CIONET Iberoamerica.

I hold an MBA with an emphasis on corporate finance from FGV-EAESP and a degree in Telecommunications Engineering from Catholic University of Santos.
